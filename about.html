A Little About Us

The AYKA Foundation campus on the Mumbai Goa Road is rapidly emerging as one of the most sought educational campus in the Maharashtra. In view of this the Institute has large number of students coming from various parts of the country. The hostel facilities at the campus are built tastefully to satisfy the personal aspiration of the residents.

Our Vision

"A home away from home" When you are away from home, it is comforting to know that the warmest and friendliest of welcome awaits you in Mumbai, the City Which Never Sleeps. We aim to be a leader in student accommodation services with a holistic living experience.

Our Mission

” Secure, hygienic and cozy.” Our main motto is to deliver efficient services and a cozy ambiance guarded with professional security and surveillance round the clock, to ensure your peace of mind, comfortable living and study environment to the residents. Our Mission to offer home-like accommodation to students who live away from their homes. We believe, however good and expensive the school/college education may be, good living conditions are very relevant for the development of the students into enlightened citizens for the future of the country. In one earnest effort to provide the best in quality, we have sought to facilitate our residents with comfort and convenience. We try our best to live up to our “More Than Just A Place To Sleep!” tag by organizing sport fests, planning fun events and by celebrating festivals with full spirit and thus keeping the “JOSH” high. Our management team is always at your service, to resolve all your issues or for your general assistance during your stay with us.
